Elocution Competition:


Our department conducted Elocution competition for the I year IT Department students under the directions given by AICTE to conduct the Induction programs to the students.

Online Webinar:


Department of Information Technology organized a Webinar on “What IT industry recruiters looking for in candidates” for the First year students Conducted by TCS.

GATE Coaching:


Department of Information technology started Gate coaching for the year 2022 on 23-10-2021.

Proficiency Module:


Department of Information Technology organized Proficiency Module on ”Testing of Electronic Devices” given by RAJA Electricals, Cuddalore.

Face Training:


Our final year students attended face training session held on 05-09-2021.

Toastmaster Club:


Our students participated Toastmaster club activity held on 21-09-2021.

Lecture And Workshop:

Guest Lecture:


A Guest Lecture on “ Data Mining and Data Ware housing” organised for III year students by speaker Chandra Mohan, VIT chennai.

Our department conducted Webinar for students to solve programs in Skill Rack on 18-09-2021. Ist year, Second year, Prefinal year and final year students attended the session.

Placement Training:


Department of information technology conducted logical reasoning and aptitude test for prefinal year students on 25-10-2021.

Essay Writing:


As a part of various induction programs conducted for the I year students of IT Department - Essay writing competition was held on the month of Feb 2021.
